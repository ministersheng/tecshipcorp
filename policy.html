Privacy Policy

How Tecship Corp collects, uses, and safeguards information submitted through this website.

Effective date: April 1, 2026

Overview

Tecship Corp ("Tecship Corp," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information we collect

When you use this website, we may collect:

- Contact details you provide through forms or email, such as name, business email, phone number, and company or institution.

- Inquiry details you voluntarily provide when requesting information or services.

- Basic technical data such as browser type, device type, and general website usage metrics collected for site performance and security.

How we use information

We use information to:

- Respond to your inquiries and requested follow-up communications.

- Evaluate and coordinate potential service support requirements.

- Maintain website reliability, performance, and security.

We do not sell your personal information.

Contact forms and communications

Information submitted through website forms is routed to Tecship Corp for response and coordination. Please do not submit classified or otherwise restricted information through this website form.

Data sharing

We may share information with service providers that help us operate this website or process communications, only to the extent required for those services. These providers are expected to handle data appropriately and securely.

Data retention

We retain submitted contact information only as long as necessary for legitimate business communication, recordkeeping, legal obligations, or security purposes.

Security

We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information submitted through this site. No transmission or storage method is guaranteed to be fully secure, and users should avoid sending sensitive restricted data through public web forms.

External links

This website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites.

Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Revisions will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
