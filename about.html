About TecShipCorp

We deliver what the mission demands. We deliver what the mission demands.

TecShipCorp is an operational logistics and base life support company serving U.S. military exercises and government operations worldwide.

Operational from Day One

TecShipCorp was built to solve a specific problem: delivering reliable base life support in complex, multi-site operational environments where the margin for error is zero. We established our presence in the Indo-Pacific because that's where the mission took us — and we've operated there since.

Today we provide full-spectrum BLS, field catering, and operational infrastructure for U.S. military exercises and government programs. Our work spans shelter systems, power generation, hygiene and sanitation, refrigerated storage, laundry, interpreter services, and daily camp sustainment — delivered to government performance standards with 4-hour emergency response across all service lines.

Our team brings operational experience across defense programs, large-scale event infrastructure, and high-headcount catering operations — disciplines that share the same core requirement: deliver exactly what was promised, on time, at scale, with no excuses.
