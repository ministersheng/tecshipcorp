Title	Mission-Critical Base Life Support Across Malaysia
BODY COPY
In July–August 2025, TecShipCorp served as the base life support contractor for Keris Strike 2025, a U.S.-Malaysia bilateral training exercise conducted under U.S. Army Pacific Pathway.
The exercise spanned approximately 30 days across multiple locations in Peninsular Malaysia, requiring coordinated delivery of infrastructure, sanitation, power, hygiene, logistics, and interpreter services for up to 700 U.S. military and civilian personnel.
All services were coordinated through designated COR interface under FAR 52.212-4 commercial terms with bilateral modification authority.
