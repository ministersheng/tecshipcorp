Our Capabilities

Demonstrated performance across defense and federal operations.

Integrated base life support for field operations

Tecship Corp provides the infrastructure and sustainment services needed to establish, operate, and close out temporary field sites. Support is aligned to the requirement, coordinated with on-site oversight, and managed to keep operations stable throughout the full performance period.

700+ Personnel Supported
6 Locations
93 CLINs Delivered
30 Days Continuous Ops

Service Lines

01
Billeting & Shelter Systems

We provide billeting tent systems sized to mission needs, including GP Medium, GP Large, and GP Extra Large configurations. Installations can include raised flooring, lighting, power access, mosquito protection, climate control, fire extinguishers, and cot layouts configured to occupancy requirements. Support covers setup, in-place maintenance, and final teardown.

02
Camp Buildout & Teardown

Services include site preparation, layout planning, structure placement, drainage control, weatherproofing, and full camp establishment in accordance with approved requirements and local site conditions. At closeout, Tecship Corp performs teardown, removal, and site restoration to the required standard, with field coordination maintained throughout all service activity.

03
Power Generation

Tecship Corp provides generator-based field power using silent-type units sized to site demand, including parallel capability where uninterrupted service is required. Support can include voltage conversion, grounding, containment, spill control, disconnect protection, and scheduled maintenance. Power operations are managed to maintain continuity, safety, and responsive support across the full operating window.

04
Hygiene & Sanitation

We furnish and service deployable latrine and handwashing systems designed for sustained field use across one or multiple locations. Support includes placement, daily pumping and recharge, disinfection, consumable replenishment, and securing of units in place. Sanitation servicing is coordinated to maintain usability, cleanliness, and continuous support for assigned personnel.

05
Shower Systems

Tecship Corp deploys portable shower systems with privacy stalls, potable water support, greywater capture, and safety features appropriate to wet-area use. Services include daily monitoring, replenishment, wastewater removal, and maintenance of safe operating conditions so shower capability remains available and serviceable throughout the performance period.

06
Waste & Medical Waste

Waste services cover routine collection, container support, hauling, and disposal for both general refuse and regulated medical waste where required. Tecship Corp manages permits, handling, transport, and disposal in accordance with applicable local requirements while maintaining scheduled pickups and restoring service areas after collection activity.

07
Laundry Service

Laundry support includes collection, cleaning, tracking, and return of personnel items within established turnaround times. Tecship Corp manages bag control, receipt documentation, transportation, and accountability so service remains orderly, traceable, and dependable during recurring field operations.

08
Flood Lighting

We provide mobile flood lighting systems for night operations, work areas, and site safety requirements. Equipment packages can include towable mast lighting, generator support, fueling, and maintenance response. Lighting assets are positioned and maintained to preserve usable illumination while minimizing disruption to ongoing operations.

09
Furnishings & Equipment

Tecship Corp supplies field furnishings and support equipment needed for billeting, dining, briefing, and work areas. Typical items include tables, chairs, cots, and fans arranged to the required layout and occupancy plan. Delivery includes positioning, setup, and integration into the overall site configuration.

10
Refrigeration & Ice

Cold chain support includes refrigerated storage, freezer capability, and ice production or delivery for field operations. Systems are maintained for continuous use with dedicated power support and responsive maintenance, helping protect perishable items and sustain daily operational requirements at designated sites.

11
Interpreter Services

Interpreter support is available for Malay-English coordination across multiple operating locations. Services can support logistics activity, training environments, briefings, and site-level communication where accurate bilingual coordination is required. Tecship Corp integrates language support to reduce execution gaps and preserve clear communication between stakeholders.

12
Emergency Response & On-Call Maintenance

Tecship Corp maintains an on-call response structure for urgent service issues across power, hygiene, shelter, refrigeration, lighting, and related support systems. Maintenance activity is coordinated around site operations, with repair or replacement actions initiated within the required response window to restore service continuity as quickly as possible.

Field catering built for sustained operations

Our food service capability is structured for recurring, high-volume feeding in operational environments. Tecship Corp supports menu planning, meal production, delivery, service-line setup, sanitation control, and accountability procedures as part of an integrated support program.

700+ Per Meal Period
FWRA Assessed & Compliant
1,150 Cal Minimum / Meal
7 Days Continuous Service

Service Lines

01
Meal Production & Controlled Preparation

Meal production is organized through contractor-managed food preparation systems designed for consistency, sanitation, and dependable throughput. Planning is aligned to approved menus, projected headcount, service windows, and dietary needs so meals can be prepared and released on time without compromising safety or readiness.

02
Menu Planning & Advance Submission

Menus are prepared in advance for review and coordination prior to service. Each plan is built around required meal composition, caloric expectations, dietary accommodation, and field practicality, helping align feeding operations with the daily schedule and reducing avoidable disruption at the point of service.

03
Breakfast Feeding Operations

Breakfast service is structured for orderly morning throughput and timely feeding before movement, training, or shift start. Support includes hot items, vegetarian options, sides, bakery or bread components, fruit, and beverages, with preparation and issue managed to preserve serviceability at delivery.

04
Dinner Feeding Operations

Dinner feeding is managed for higher-volume evening service with complete meal presentation, controlled line flow, and dependable timing. Standard support includes hot entrees, a vegetarian option, side dishes, bread items, salad components, dessert, and beverages, all prepared to maintain quality and sanitation through the service window.

05
Temperature-Controlled Transport & Delivery

Meals are transported under controlled conditions from preparation sites to designated service points. Hot and cold items are managed to maintain safe holding conditions during staging, movement, and setup, with delivery sequencing coordinated around site access and required feeding windows so meals arrive ready for immediate issue.

06
Serving Line Setup & Meal Issue

Tecship Corp provides the labor, equipment, tables, utensils, disposables, condiments, and support materials required to run complete serving lines. Lines are arranged for orderly throughput, accurate meal issue, and reduced congestion during peak periods while allowing clear oversight at the service point.

07
Food Safety & Hazard Control

Food safety controls are applied across receiving, storage, preparation, cooking, transport, holding, and final distribution. Operations are managed to reduce contamination risk, maintain temperature compliance, and preserve disciplined handling practices through the full chain of custody.

08
Sanitization & Cleaning Control

Cleaning and sanitization procedures are maintained across food-contact surfaces, utensils, containers, and service equipment throughout the period of performance. Sanitizer use, contact times, ware-washing, and surface cleaning are managed as active controls to support sanitary operations and inspection readiness.

09
Dietary Accommodation & Restricted Meal Support

Meal operations can accommodate vegetarian requirements, declared food allergies, and religious dietary restrictions within the broader feeding plan. These needs are addressed during menu development and service execution so personnel can be supported safely without disrupting the primary service line.

10
Personnel Hygiene, Appearance & Conduct Standards

Catering personnel operate under defined hygiene, appearance, and conduct standards appropriate to government-supported food service environments. Hand hygiene, glove use, hair restraint, illness exclusion, attire compliance, and disciplined food-handling practices are maintained as baseline operating requirements throughout performance.

11
Headcount Verification & Meal Accountability

Meal issue is coordinated with required headcount and accountability procedures to preserve billing integrity, service accuracy, and clear documentation. Tecship Corp supports orderly counting, controlled issuance, and auditable records where oversight personnel require meal verification during recurring operations.

12
Quality Assurance, Inspection Readiness & Corrective Action

Catering performance is managed against measurable service objectives covering sanitation, menu execution, serving throughput, accountability, and availability of required items. Operations are maintained in inspection-ready condition, with corrective action initiated promptly when deficiencies are identified, helping preserve continuity and confidence in contract performance.
