Mission Overview

A multi-week retail activation delivered inside an active mall environment

Slide the Curve was a public-facing water slide activation staged within the Curve, combining temporary attraction infrastructure, branded public presentation, registration flow, and daily guest handling inside an active retail environment.

Tecship Corp supported the project end-to-end across branding setup, ticket handling, safety controls, operational staffing, day-to-day execution, and final teardown throughout the full event period from 3 to 22 September 2024.

Scope of work

End-to-end delivery across branding, ticketing, safety, guest flow, and closeout.

01 - Branding Setup

Installed branded event elements and presentation components to align the attraction with the Curve's retail environment and campaign identity.

02 - Ticketing & Entry Flow

Supported ticket handling, registration flow, and orderly guest intake to keep entry processing smooth during public operating hours.

03 - Safety Readiness

Maintained daily safety controls and operational readiness to support public participation across the full activation period.

04 - Day-to-Day Operations

Handled daily event support in a live mall setting, coordinating guest movement, operating rhythm, and front-of-house execution.

05 - Teardown & Site Reset

Closed out the activation through teardown and site reset after the operating window, ensuring a clean handback at the end of the project period.

Operating Priorities

Built for public flow, retail compatibility, and daily operating consistency.

Because Slide the Curve ran inside a mall environment, execution depended on more than attraction setup alone. The project had to balance guest excitement, public safety, venue presentation, and day-to-day consistency over a multi-week operating period.

Tecship Corp's role was to help keep the activation functioning as a coordinated public experience, with branding, ticketing, safety, operations, and closeout all working together as one delivery model.
