Mission Overview

Mission-critical base life support across Malaysia

In July-August 2025, Tecship Corp served as the base life support contractor for a U.S.-Malaysia bilateral training exercise conducted under U.S. Army Pacific Pathway.

The exercise spanned approximately 30 days across multiple locations in Peninsular Malaysia, requiring coordinated delivery of infrastructure, sanitation, power, hygiene, logistics, and interpreter services for up to 700 U.S. military and civilian personnel.

All services ran through one accountable channel to the customer team under commercial terms, with scope aligned through the exercise chain of authority as needs evolved.

Interpreter Services

Tecship Corp provided 5 simultaneous interpreters across 4 locations - Penang, Lumut, Ipoh LSA, and Penang return rotation - for the duration of the exercise.

Interpreters supported basic logistics coordination, tactical field training, and medical training lanes, with a contractual error rate threshold below 1%.
