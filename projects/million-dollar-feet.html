Mission Overview

Nationwide youth football program delivery across multi-city trials and final selection

Million Dollar Feet was a youth football talent identification program conducted across Penang, Kuantan, Kuching, Johor Bahru, and Kuala Lumpur, drawing strong participation from aspiring players between 11 and 14 years old.

As co-founder and operator of the project, Tecship Corp led event setup, participant movement, venue coordination, and on-ground execution across multiple selection locations leading into the final round in Kuala Lumpur.

Operating Priorities

Built for scale, participant control, and a credible national selection process.

Million Dollar Feet depended on more than branding or one-day event support. The program required operational consistency across multiple cities, strong participant handling, and the ability to move from local selection events into a final-stage national decision point.

Tecship Corp's role as co-founder and operator meant maintaining continuity across structure, delivery, and on-ground execution so the program could function as a credible pathway for youth football talent identification.
