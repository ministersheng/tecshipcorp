Mission Overview

End-to-end marathon delivery across race operations, participant flow, and event-day execution

Run Out Pahang Marathon 2018 was held on 11 March 2018 in Kuantan, featuring 42km, 21km, and 10km race distances and requiring disciplined race-day coordination across participant movement, route support, and event timing.

Tecship Corp delivered the event end-to-end, working across race operations, participant experience, and field execution, with visible support from local stakeholders including participation from the Pahang royal family.

Scope of work

Race-day delivery across route coordination, participant handling, and event management.

01 - Event Organization

Organized the event end-to-end, coordinating race structure, participant-facing setup, and operational planning across the full program.

02 - Route Operations

Managed course execution for the 42km, 21km, and 10km race formats, supporting movement, timing, and route-side coordination.

03 - Participant Services

Supported the runner experience through organized participant flow, event-day support points, and clear operating control across the venue footprint.

04 - Stakeholder Coordination

Worked alongside local stakeholders and public figures tied to the event environment, helping maintain smooth event delivery and public visibility.

05 - Event-Day Execution

Maintained on-ground operational control across setup, live running conditions, and finish-area management on race day.
